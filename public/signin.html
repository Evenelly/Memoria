Privacy Policy for Memoria
Effective Date: 2025-04-04
Welcome to Memoria. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Memoria, we collect the following information:
- Username & Password – when you create an account, you provide a username and password.
- Content You Create – such as posts, comments, or other input you share on the site.
- Cookie-Based User ID – we store your user ID in a cookie so we can personalize your experience (e.g., showing your friends, posts, etc.).
We do not collect email addresses or other personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide access to your account
- Display your posts, comments, and friends
- Keep your session active while browsing the site
- Improve and secure the performance of Memoria
We do not sell or share your data with any third-party services.

3. How Your Data is Stored

All your data is securely stored in our MongoDB database. Passwords are hashed, meaning we never store them in a readable format. Your data is never shared outside of Memoria.

4. Cookies

We use one cookie — a unique ID linked to your account. This cookie is essential for logging in and browsing Memoria, as it tells us who you are so we can load your content. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

Memoria is not intended for children under 15 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from anyone under 15. If you believe a child under 15 has used our service, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- View the data we store about you
- Request deletion of your account and all related content
- Correct inaccurate information
To make a request, just send us an email.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or privacy concerns, you can reach us at:
evelina.carlsson@memoria.com
